My University Journey

Click below to read my full journey...

1. First Day of University

I got admission in UET Lahore Faisalabad Campus in Computer Engineering (first batch). I had big dreams and excitement. Teachers were supportive and guided us well.

2. Hostel Life

I lived in a hall with 17 students. Initially interaction was low. My best hostel friend is Usman Ali and also Fakhar Abbas. Mess food and routine were challenging.

3. First Week Experience

Started lectures and labs. Made friends Fakhar Abbas, Ahmad Raza and Adil Hassan. Group study helped a lot.

4. Mid Exams

No free days between exams. Each paper was 90 minutes. Programming and Calculus were easy and I scored full marks.

5. After Mids

Weekly assignments, quizzes and lab work (AICT, Physics). Spent 3 hours in labs and improved skills.

6. Sports

I prefer indoor games, especially chess. Participated in departmental competition and got good scores.

7. Machine Learning Project

Created loan approval ML model using Python. Used features like salary, loan history. Presented successfully.

8. Final Exams

Got 1st position in whole department with 3.941 GPA. Enjoyed 15 days holidays.

9. Second Semester

Subjects: OOP (C#), DBMS, DLD, Calculus II. Learned programming and databases. Friends include Usman Ali.

10. Online Classes

Due to petrol issues classes shifted online. Faced challenges but now returned for mid exams.
